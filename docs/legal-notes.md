# Legal and Ethical Notes

This project is a small MVP for lead research and portfolio demonstration.

## Rules

- Do not bypass CAPTCHAs or anti-bot systems.
- Do not use stolen cookies, sessions, or private headers.
- Do not collect private personal data.
- Do not scrape aggressively.
- Keep limits low for MVP testing.
- Prefer official APIs, allowed exports, or manual demo data.
- Store only business contact data that is publicly available.

## MVP Limit

The default MVP limit is 10 companies per run.

## Public Repository Safety

The public version must contain only:

- demo data;
- placeholders;
- safe skeleton code;
- cleaned screenshots;
- no real API keys;
- no real lead exports.
